Hello, my name is Guillaume Lauga.

I am currently a PhD student at the École Normale Supérieure de Lyon since November 2021 under the supervision of Paulo Goncalves , Elisa Riccietti and Nelly Pustelnik .

I am part of the Inria DANTE team of the LIP laboratory and my PhD is fully funded by Inria.
